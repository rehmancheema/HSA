HSA Lawyers

HSA Lawyers is a boutique law firm with a clear focus: M&A, Private Equity, Venture Capital and corporate law aimed at entrepreneurs.


Founded by former 'big law' lawyers, HSA Lawyers (formerly Haagstate) has been providing high-quality legal advice to private equity houses, venture capital investors, family funds, corporates and DGAs since 2008.


Our experienced lawyers are result-oriented, use an efficient approach and have an eye for what is important to successfully complete a transaction.


HSA Lawyers has a strong network in the most relevant jurisdictions and regularly cooperates as a Dutch law specialist in a broader (international) team on cross-border transactions.

What we do

From (vendor) due diligence, drafting or reviewing contracts to the realization of acquisitions, restructurings or joint ventures: HSA Lawyers provides entrepreneurs, investors and management with advice and support in the field of mergers and acquisitions, setting up private equity funds, investment structuring and corporate law issues. If the necessary knowledge is not available in-house, we also have an extensive national and international network at our disposal, enabling us to involve the right experts in our advice.
